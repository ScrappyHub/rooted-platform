# ROOTED — Parent & Guardian Kids Safety Charter

This charter exists for parents, guardians, educators, and families.

ROOTED treats child safety as constitutional law, not a feature.

---

## What Kids Mode Is

Kids Mode is a protected educational environment for young users.
It exists to allow exploration of:

- Farms
- Food systems
- Nature
- Community helpers
- Safety education
- Art, science, and emotional learning

---

## What Kids Mode Is NOT

Kids Mode does NOT allow:

- Messaging with strangers
- Purchases
- Donations
- Bookings
- RFQs, bids, or commercial tools
- Advertising of any kind
- Data exploitation
- Fear-based or violent content

---

## Parental Control Doctrine

- Kids Mode requires adult consent to activate.
- Adults may deactivate Kids Mode at any time.
- Kids cannot override adult controls.
- No backdoors exist inside the system.

---

## Media & Content Protections

- Kids content is filtered by safety overlays.
- Kids content is filtered by moderation approval.
- Kids content never bypasses youth filters.
- Unsafe content cannot be injected by users.

---

## Data Protection for Kids

- No behavioral tracking of children
- No marketing profiles
- No monetization
- No messaging

Educational interaction only.

---

## Reporting & Support

If you ever see content that feels unsafe:

- It can be reported immediately.
- Human review is required.
- Repeat violations result in permanent restriction.

---

ROOTED is committed to protecting childhood from platform harm.
Kids are not users to monetize—they are people to protect.
